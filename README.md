# Suppliers-Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze supplier performance, financing activities, and auto contract trends. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of supplier contributions, financing amounts, and sales distribution while enabling users to explore data through dynamic filters.

Key Features
📊 KPI Cards displaying:

Total Auto Contracts

Total Finance Amount

Number of Suppliers

Toyota Contracts

Non-Toyota Contracts

Used Vehicle Contracts

📈 Top 5 suppliers ranked by total sales.

📊 Auto contracts breakdown by financing sector.

🥧 Sales distribution by vehicle type (Toyota, Non-Toyota, and Used).

📉 Monthly Finance Amount compared with the previous year.

📉 Monthly Auto Contracts compared with the previous year.

🔎 Interactive filters for Year, Month, Sector, and Sales Type, allowing users to drill down into specific business segments.

Purpose
This dashboard enables business stakeholders to monitor supplier performance, evaluate financing trends, compare monthly performance against the previous year, and identify key suppliers and sectors contributing to overall business growth. The interactive design supports data-driven decision-making by providing a clear view of operational and financial performance.

Tools Used: Power BI, Power Query, DAX
